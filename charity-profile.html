Habitat for Humanity of Greater Los Angeles

Location: Los Angeles, CA

Habitat for Humanity of Greater Los Angeles (Habitat LA) partners with hardworking, low-income families and individuals to build and repair homes. We provide a hand up, not a handout, creating long-term investments in the future.

Volunteer Opportunities

  • Home Building Projects: Assist in the construction of new homes for families in need.
  • Repair and Renovation: Help fix and restore homes for families facing challenges.
  • Fundraising Events: Organize and participate in charity events to raise funds for Habitat LA's mission.
